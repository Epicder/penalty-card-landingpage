PENALTY CARD
SHOOTOUT

Think, Choose, SHOOT!

Penalty Card Shootout is a penalty shootout game inspired by football cards. Two users select football teams with a total of 8 players and compete in a penalty shootout. Each player has special skills reflected on their cards, which influence the outcomes of the penalties. Similarly, it is the user who has the power to choose where to shoot or save.
